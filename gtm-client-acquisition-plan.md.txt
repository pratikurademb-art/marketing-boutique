# GTM Client Acquisition Plan — Marketing Boutique
**Goal:** Land 1 paying B2B SaaS client for GTM Engineering Services
**Offer:** Agentic AI Lead Enrichment + Outbound Starter
**Price:** $750 fixed | 150 enriched leads | 14-day delivery
**Plan Duration:** 6 Weeks
**Start Date:** April 7, 2026

---

## 🎯 Target Customer (ICP)

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| Company Type | B2B SaaS |
| Stage | Early-stage ($0–$2M ARR) |
| Team Size | 1–20 employees |
| Location | United States |
| Pain Point | No SDR team, no outbound system |
| Decision Maker | Founder / Head of Sales / GTM Lead |

---

## 📦 The Offer

**Agentic AI Lead Enrichment + Outbound Starter**

- 150 leads enriched with firmographics, technographics & buying signals
- ICP-fit scoring using Clay
- Personalized outbound email sequences (ready to deploy)
- Delivered in 14 days
- Fixed price: $750

---

## 🗓️ 6-Week Execution Plan

### Week 1 — Setup & Targeting (April 7–13)
- [ ] Finalize ICP criteria
- [ ] Build lead list using Apollo.io (150 contacts)
- [ ] Set up Clay workspace for enrichment
- [ ] Define outbound messaging framework

### Week 2 — Enrichment & Personalization (April 14–20)
- [ ] Run all 150 leads through Clay enrichment
- [ ] Add technographic + buying signal data
- [ ] Score leads by ICP fit (High / Medium / Low)
- [ ] Write 3 personalized email templates

### Week 3 — Outreach Begins (April 21–27)
- [ ] Launch LinkedIn outreach via Sales Navigator
- [ ] Send connection requests to top 50 ICP leads
- [ ] Begin cold email sequence to remaining leads
- [ ] Track replies in Google Sheets

### Week 4 — Follow Up & Optimize (April 28 – May 4)
- [ ] Follow up with non-responders (Day 3 & Day 7)
- [ ] Refine messaging based on reply data
- [ ] Book at least 2–3 discovery calls
- [ ] Qualify leads using BANT framework

### Week 5 — Discovery Calls & Pitching (May 5–11)
- [ ] Conduct discovery calls
- [ ] Present the $750 pilot offer
- [ ] Send proposal/deck after each call
- [ ] Handle objections and follow up

### Week 6 — Close & Deliver (May 12–18)
- [ ] Close 1 paid pilot client
- [ ] Kick off 14-day delivery
- [ ] Deliver 150 enriched leads + outbound sequences
- [ ] Collect feedback and case study material
