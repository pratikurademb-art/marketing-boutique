### Pain Points

1. Current outbound is not working — replies have dropped, generic SDR plays burned out
2. Need extra help — no bandwidth on existing team
3. Tried agencies, didn't work out (poor fit, generic templates)
4. Can't hire a GTM Engineer in-house (too expensive, hiring market tight)
5. Curious about AI in marketing/sales but no in-house expertise

### Qualification Definition

A meeting is **qualified** when 3 of these 5 are true:

1. Pipeline motion is established (inbound / outbound / founder-network)
2. Outbound stack is in use (Apollo, Smartlead, Clay, etc.)
3. AI usage in marketing/sales is on their radar
4. Hiring vs. outsourcing decision is active
5. Has tried agencies before (positive or negative — both are signal)

Plus all three foundations: **decision-maker title + active outbound problem + budget signal ($1K+ feasible).**

## 📡 Outbound System — 5-Touch Parallel

### Voice Guardrails

- Lead with confidence — no "happy to" hedging on every line
- Talk their lingo — "pipeline," "new logos," "deals" (not "outbound that's flat")
- No "Pratik" sign-off on every message — name lives in email signature, not as a self-introduction tic
- No client success stories yet — skills + signal-based reads are the edge
- Team positioning: **"GTM engineer team — Bay Area + India"**
- Value prop in their language: **"2–5 new logos to pipeline / month"**

### Channels

| # | Channel | Trigger | Char Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LinkedIn invite + 170-char note | Day 1 | 170 |
| 2 | LinkedIn DM after accept | On accept | None |
| 3 | Cold email (Apollo/Clay-enriched) | Day 1, parallel | None |
| 4 | Free InMail to Premium profiles | Day 3 if not accepted | 300 (design cap, not LinkedIn's 1,900) |
| 5 | LinkedIn company page DM | Day 5 if not accepted | None |
| ➕ | Comment on recent LinkedIn post | Day 0 (warm-up) + ongoing | N/A |

### 14-Day Sequence Per Prospect

```
Day -2 to 0   CHEAT SHEET BUILD
              Pratik researches: tools, recent posts, hiring signals,
              mutual connections, fundraise/launch, ICP-fit score,
              two angle insights for the DM.

Day 0         WARM-UP: Comment on most recent LinkedIn post
              (genuine, value-add — not "great post 🔥").

Day 1         TOUCH 1A: LinkedIn invite + 170-char note
              TOUCH 1B: Cold email (parallel)

Day 3         IF Premium AND not accepted → free InMail
              IF accepted → DM intro with cheat-sheet hook

Day 5         IF still not accepted → LinkedIn company page DM
                                    + Email touch 2 (different angle)

Day 7         IF accepted but no reply → DM follow-up (AI question)

Day 10        Email touch 3 (case-study angle / signal-based)

Day 14        Break-up Part 1: humor + qualify the rejection

Day 18        IF no reply to break-up Part 1 → Final close
              IF reply → Pratik writes custom Part 2 per response type
```

### Drop-In Copy

**1. LinkedIn invite note (159 chars):**
> Hey {{first}}, building {{company}} — saw you're hiring on the GTM side. We run a small GTM engineer studio. How's outbound looking for you right now?

**2. LinkedIn DM intro (after accept):**
> Thanks for accepting, {{first}}.
>
> Context — we're a GTM engineer team across Bay Area + India, working with early-stage founders to add **2–5 new logos to pipeline every month**.
>
> Pulled a quick read on {{company}} based on your stack ({{tool_1}}, {{tool_2}}) + recent {{signal}}. Two angles I'd push on if you were a client: **{{insight_1}}** and **{{insight_2}}**.
>
> Happy to chat more?

**3. Cold email (Day 1, parallel):**
> **Subject:** outbound at {{company}} — quick read
>
> {{first}} — keeping this short. We're a GTM engineer team (Bay Area + India). Saw {{signal}} at {{company}}.
>
> Most early-stage founders we talk to are dealing with one of three things: replies have dropped, the agency didn't deliver, or a GTM hire is off the table. Recognizable?
>
> Can send a free 5-point teardown of your current GTM — useful?

**4. Free InMail (Premium profiles, Day 3, before accept):**
> {{first}} — no expectation of reply.
>
> GTM engineer team across Bay Area + India here. Quick read on {{company}}: two things I'd change about your current outbound based on {{signal}} + your stack — **{{insight_1}}** and **{{insight_2}}**.
>
> Worth 15 minutes if it lands.

**5. Company page DM (Day 5, if no accept):**
> Hey team — tried reaching {{first}} directly. GTM engineer studio (Bay Area + India), helping early-stage founders add 2–5 new logos to pipeline / month. Free GTM audit available for {{first}} if useful — happy to share the teardown.

**6. Day-7 follow-up DM (post-accept, no reply):**
> {{first}} — quick one. How are you using AI in marketing / sales right now? Asking because most founders we talk to are either not started or stuck at prompt-level stuff.

**7. Break-up Part 1 (Day 14, humor pattern-interrupt + qualify rejection):**
> {{first}} — quick interruption: my CFO says you owe us **$500 trillion** for the unread messages (we're flexible on terms 🙃).
>
> Real talk — I've messaged a few times and got radio silence. Two seconds of honesty: is this a **wrong time**, **wrong fit**, or **wrong message** for you?
>
> Even one word back helps me not waste your inbox next quarter.

**8. Break-up Part 2 (only sent based on reply to Part 1):**

| Their reply | Pratik responds with |
|---|---|
| **"Wrong time"** | Permission to circle back in 60–90 days. Logs follow-up date. |
| **"Wrong fit"** | One-question dig: *"Got it — out of curiosity, what about us doesn't fit? Helps me sharpen."* |
| **"Wrong message"** | One-question dig: *"Fair. What would have been the right hook? Genuinely asking."* |
| **No reply to Part 1** | Day 18 final close: *"All good {{first}} — closing the loop. Door's open if outbound or AI agents become a priority. Best with {{company}}."* |

### Qualifying Questions (use after they reply)

When **3 of 5 land convincingly = qualified**:

1. **"How are you driving pipeline today — inbound, outbound, founder-network?"**
2. **"What tools are you running for outbound — Apollo, Smartlead, Clay, anything else?"**
3. **"How are you using AI in marketing / sales right now?"**
4. **"Is hiring a GTM person on the table, or are you outsourcing that bandwidth?"**
5. **"Have you tried agencies before — how did that go?"**

## 📐 Metrics Definitions

- **Acceptance rate** = `accepts / invites_sent` — measured 14 days after invite
- **Reply rate** = `substantive_replies / accepts` — substantive = anything beyond emoji or "thanks"
- **Qualified** = `qualification_score >= 3` (3 of 5 questions answered convincingly Y)
- **Qualified meeting** = booked + qualification pre-confirmed in DM **before** the calendar invite
- **Pilot closed** = signed agreement + first invoice paid
